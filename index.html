About

Minna Company is producer of industrial pallets, furniture of pallet and sofas. The pallets used for gardening furniture are made of materials of highest quality, which guarantee their great vision and long-term usage. We work by individual projects as well.

Minna Company LTD is Bulgarian firm focused mainly on production of wooden pallets and details. The firm was founded in 1991 with subject woodworking and production of wooden stuff. Since 2005 we are mainly producing any kinds of pallets and details under the customer's wish. We have made from the smallest to the biggest pallets and grates. The pallets and details are made of first class coniferous material. We have our own thermal cameras as well as ISO certificate and standard 15.
